The Best movies according to Shraddha

My Top 5 Movies.

Sholay (1975)

A definitive action-adventure film where a retired police officer hires two outlaws to capture a ruthless bandit who destroyed his family.

3 Idiots (2009)

A comedy-drama exploring the pressures of the Indian education system, following two friends searching for their long-lost companion who inspired them to think differently.

Mughal-E-Azam (1960)

A historical epic depicting the forbidden love story between Prince Salim and court dancer Anarkali, defying the emperor Akbar.

Taare Zameen Par (Like Stars on Earth) (2007)

A heartwarming drama about an art teacher who discovers a struggling student has dyslexia and helps him overcome his learning challenges.

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995)

A definitive romance film about a young man striving to win over the traditional family of the woman he loves during a trip across Europe and India.
